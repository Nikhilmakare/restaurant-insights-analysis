# restaurant-insights-analysis

## Project Overview

Restaurant Insights Analysis is a data analysis project focused on exploring restaurant trends, customer preferences, pricing patterns, ratings, and service availability using Python. This project was developed as part of the Data Analysis Internship at Cognifyz Technologies.

The objective of this project is to extract meaningful business insights from restaurant data through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization techniques.

---

## Problem Statement

The food industry generates massive amounts of data related to customer behavior, restaurant ratings, cuisines, pricing, and delivery services. Analyzing this data helps businesses understand market trends, customer preferences, and operational opportunities.

This project aims to analyze restaurant data to uncover actionable insights that can support business decisions.

---

## Objectives

* Identify the most common cuisines and their popularity.
* Analyze city-wise restaurant distribution and ratings.
* Study the price range distribution of restaurants.
* Evaluate the impact of online delivery on restaurant ratings.
* Analyze rating patterns and customer voting behavior.
* Identify popular cuisine combinations.
* Perform geographic analysis using latitude and longitude.
* Detect restaurant chains and evaluate their performance.

---

## Key Insights

* North Indian, Chinese, and Fast Food are among the most common cuisines.
* Major cities have a significantly higher concentration of restaurants.
* Mid-range pricing categories dominate the market.
* Online delivery services influence customer convenience and engagement.
* Most restaurants fall within the average-to-good rating range.
* Certain cuisine combinations perform better in customer ratings.
* Restaurant clusters are highly concentrated in urban areas.
* Chain restaurants show strong popularity and customer trust.
